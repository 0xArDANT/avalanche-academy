By the end of this course, students will:

- Understand the challenges of cross-chain communication
- Know what separates Avalanche Warp Messaging from other cross-chain communication protocols
- Understand the differences between Avalanche Warp Messaging and Teleporter
- Apply their knowledge by building cross-Subnet dApps, such as asset bridges

Overall, this course aims to provide an advanced understanding of Teleporter. By completing this course, students will be better prepared to build advanced cross-Subnet blockchain applications.
